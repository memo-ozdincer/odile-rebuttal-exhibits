\section*{AgentDojo --- the real injections ODILE trained on (verbatim)}

\footnotesize
These are the exact attacker goals in ODILE's training traces, per suite. Compare them against the held-out InjecAgent and TensorTrust lists: the training attacks are AgentDojo banking money-transfer, data exfiltration via AgentDojo tools, and the like. The held-out benchmarks share no surface form, domain, or tooling with these --- which is what makes the cross-benchmark transfer a generalization claim rather than a memorization one.
