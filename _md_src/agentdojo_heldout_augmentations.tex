\section*{AgentDojo held-out attack augmentations: same goal, surface form the adapter never trained on}

\footnotesize
ODILE trains on a narrow slice of AgentDojo injections (banking money-transfer / data-exfil under the \texttt{important\_instructions} \texttt{<INFORMATION>} wrapper). It is \textbf{evaluated} on 13 attack families, most of which keep the attacker goal fixed and only \emph{transform the surface form} the adapter saw: the authority wrapper stripped off, the payload restyled as a polite compliance email, base64 framing, a fake kernel log, the model's own injected \texttt{Thought:/Action:} monologue, or a deprecated-protocol notice with zero imperative verbs. Because the goal is held fixed and only the wrapper changes, these are out-of-distribution rewrappings, not memorized strings. ODILE reaches $0\%$ ASR on 12 of 13 families (mean $0.013\%$) at Llama-3.3-70B; the undefended baseline averages $14.04\%$.

\begin{slot}[colback=labgrey!35]{10--13 $+$ extended set \;---\; also-registered held-out rewrappings (same \texttt{\{goal\}} slot)}
Registered alongside the families above in \fpath{odile/src/odile/attacks/baseline_attacks.py}:
\begin{itemize}
\item \texttt{shadowleak\_explicit} \;---\; ShadowLeak polite-email framing $+$ an explicit tool-call sequence (\texttt{\{tools\}}); no \texttt{<INFORMATION>}, no imperatives.
\item \texttt{compositional\_shadowmono} \;---\; ShadowLeak authority framing fused with the FakeMonologue \texttt{Thought:/Action:} structure; a combination of two families, neither seen in training.
\item \texttt{longctx\_shadowleak} / \texttt{longctx\_fakemono} \;---\; $\sim$1,800 tokens of benign business filler prepended before the body (attention-dilution / position-robustness stress).
\item \texttt{tool\_schema\_redefinition} \;---\; reframes a legit tool's documented behavior so the harmful goal is presented as the tool's ``canonical effect''; no instruction shape.
\item \texttt{trickle\_relay} \;---\; splits the attack into a ``reference value'' half and an ``apply it later'' half to defeat per-message scanners.
\item \texttt{kernel\_authority\_mimicry} \;---\; injection styled as an out-of-band machine-emitted broker message (\texttt{<<KMSG ring=0 \ldots>>}); no English instruction shape.
\item \texttt{refuse\_to\_refuse} \;---\; a meta-instruction about response style (suppress confirmations) with the harmful goal recast as an onboarding-approved ``standing item.''
\item \texttt{paraphrase\_chain} \;---\; the same goal stated three times as semantically-equivalent paraphrases to dilute per-string surface matching.
\end{itemize}
\end{slot}
