\section*{AgentDyn --- how it differs from AgentDojo (real, verbatim)}
\footnotesize AgentDojo (ODILE's training source) has 4 fixed suites: banking, slack, travel, workspace. \textbf{AgentDyn} is a separate benchmark on \textbf{3 suites that do not exist in AgentDojo --- GitHub, Daily-Life, Shopping}. The attack genuinely lands on base here (\textbf{30.0\% ASR}, n=80, trace-validated); ODILE drives it to $\approx$0.\par\medskip
